About Us

Jacobson Robotics is a pioneering robotics startup headquartered in San Diego, California.

We are driven by the belief that technology should allow individuals and organizations to tackle real-world challenges. To achieve this, we are focused on developing low-cost, highly adaptable, and incredibly robust robots. Our goal is to enable their use across a wide spectrum of applications, with minimal setup costs.

At Jacobson Robotics, we are not just pushing the boundaries of technology; we are tearing them down to bring about the future - to make the robotic worlds of science fiction a reality. Join us in this exciting adventure towards a future where technology knows no bounds.

Our Products

Rex Quadruped

Rex is our experimental quadrupedal robot, the first in a line of low-cost, modifiable, and durable robots. Designed to be versatile and affordable, Rex is poised to revolutionize robotics by offering adaptability and durability in a compact package.
